Audit and Assurance

Our audit and assurance service offerings are based on a complete understanding of the clients’ business specifics, industry peculiarities, and the applicable laws. The audit approach focuses on compliance, the nature and requirement of the audit, and key risk issues involved.

Our audit and assurance solutions range from statutory audits, internal audits, tax audits, transfer pricing audits, management audits, and more. These audits aim to improve the clients' business financial and operational performance.

Statutory Legal Audits

We conduct statutory legal and regulatory compliance audits for filing of financial results. These audits ensure compliance with regulations, assess control strength, confirm the accounting treatment of transactions, and provide an independent review of reported information.

Tax Audits

We annually serve clients in tax audits under various provisions of the tax laws. Our clients include companies, medium and small businesses, partnership firms, nonprofits, SACCOs, CBOs, and high-net-worth individuals. Our specialized audit teams conduct the audits effectively and diligently.

Internal Audits / Internal Controls

Internal audits are designed to meet client-specific requirements but generally aim to provide assurance on the effectiveness of controls and processes.
