Farm Accounting

Assisting Agricultural producers (Farmers) manage and grow their business. Murey Yego & Associates has vast experience in the farming industry and understand the unique environment of these family businesses. Our clients consist of a diverse range of Agribusiness including livestock and grain, Poultry, orchard farming and aquaculture.

We provide primary production with Proper Record Keeping, taxation and accounting advice as well as advice for those who wish to grow their business by diversification and/or purchasing additional land or resources as well as Value addition. There are many variables in primary production including world markets, weather, health and availability of livestock and worldwide commodity prices that need to be considered as part of running a successful agribusiness. Our services include:

  • Business accounting and tax compliance
  • Cash flow and financing assistance
  • Ownership structures and restructures
  • Superannuation
  • Seasonal planning
  • Assist with farm sales and purchases documentation and compliance
  • Succession and Estate planning

We are ready to guide you through the vast range of challenges primary industry presents and assist you in managing your business more effectively and profitably.
